Process

My process to develop your website

Step 1 – Project Definition

Click on the contact button in the right corner of your screen or at the end of the page, and complete the form. Tell me about your project, your business, your goals (deadline,number of pages, what you need, etc.) I will get back to you in 48 hours. Together we will build a plan, the timeline and budget.

Step 2 – Web design & SEO strategy

Starting form your ideas, your type of business, and the latest trends, I will design your website in Figma. This design will be then sent to you for validation. If modifications are needed, I'll update the design until you're happy with it.

Step 3 – Webflow or Wordpress development

ll develop your website using Webflow or WordPress. It is also possible to use Webflow to create the site's design and then transfer it into Wordpress. All the classes, headers,.. will be clearly named in order for you to update your website, if needed. I can also assist you in selecting a web host and a domain name.

Step 4 - Training & analytics

Once your site is live, I will train you on its maintenance, updates, firsts posts, and the use of traffic analysis plugins.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take ?

A website takes from 1 week to 8 weeks, to develop on average. Using no code or low code allows me to save time and have a quicker turnaround time.

How much does it cost ?

Every website is different, so it is difficult to give you a price without knowing your project. For most websites, prices range from 800€ for a landing page to 4 000€ for an e-commerce website. If you are a non-profit or are tight on a budget, do not hesitate to contact me. We can discuss your project and make it work within your price range.

Why design and animation matter ?

Sure, having a visually appealing website is a goal in and of itself.
However, web design is more than that; it is also about creating simple, accessible, and intuitive websites. Your website's animation, and interactions improves the user experience, keeps them interested, help you stand out, and get your message across.
